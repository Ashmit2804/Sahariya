Introduction

A Community at the Heart of Culture

The Sahariya community of Rajasthan represents one of India's most culturally rich yet underrepresented Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs). This platform brings together visual, performative, and oral traditions through long-term ethnographic fieldwork and collaborative documentation.

This digital archive documents Mandana paintings, ritual practices, performances, and oral narratives—not as static artefacts, but as living expressions of ecological knowledge, spirituality, and community life.
